About Us

Discover more about our mission, values, and the team behind "FND Verify".

Our Mission

At FND Verify, we are committed to combating the spread of misinformation by providing a reliable and intelligent platform that detects and verifies the authenticity of news articles. Our aim is to foster a well-informed society by leveraging cutting-edge technologies in machine learning and natural language processing.

Our Vision

To create a world where truth prevails, enabling individuals and organizations to make informed decisions without falling victim to fake news and misinformation.

Our Technology

Our platform integrates advanced machine learning algorithms like XGBoost and Natural Language Processing (NLP) to classify and verify news authenticity. Using the power of frameworks such as Django for backend development and Bootstrap for responsive design, we ensure an efficient and user-friendly experience. Data insights are enhanced by analyzing user feedback and integrating modern analytics tools.
